Dear Jessica,
Thanks for being my inspiration through the past and up until now. I'll never be able to forget how you always brighten up my day whenever I see you. Just one smile of yours definitely melts my heart away. Although we don't talk that often, I hope you will get to know me better in the future. I wish there will come a time wherein our paths will meet again so that I can express more of my love for you.
