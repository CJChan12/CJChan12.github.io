Dear Chan family,
I can't show how much I love and care about you guys. I don't know if I'll be able to repay everything you have done to me. Growing up, I always looked behind your back and I wasn't disappointed. You raised me into a fine young man and that is something I'm pressured to do after I become a father. I will always thank you for giving me the gift of life and for always being there beside me through thick and thin.
